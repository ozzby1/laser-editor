What does this tool do?

Photo Engraving Online converts a regular photo into a rasterized black-and-white image optimized for CO2 or diode laser engraving.

Recommended DPI by material
DPI Best for Note
72 DPI Leather, fabrics, very rough wood Low detail, fast engraving
150 DPI Soft wood (pine, linden), OSB Good result for larger formats
254 DPI Hard wood (oak, beech), plywood Good balance between detail and speed
300 DPI ✓ Glass, stone, slate, MDF Default value – suitable for most projects
500 DPI Acrylic, coated metals, ceramics High detail, great for portraits
750 DPI Anodized aluminum, cast acrylic Maximum detail, slower engraving
How to choose the correct output size?

The output size in mm should exactly match the real physical size of the engraving area on your material.

Example

ℹ️ Formula: width in px = (width in mm ÷ 25.4) × DPI
